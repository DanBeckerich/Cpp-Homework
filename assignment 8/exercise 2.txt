Start by locating the stairs.
move to the base of the stairs. 
for total number of stairs devided by the number of legs.
	for each leg
		raise the current foot until it clears the next stair;
		move the foot overtop the stair;
		lower the foot until it touches the stair.
		shift weight onto the stair.
		lift body with current leg.
		center weight ontop of current foot

this algorithm will work for any number of stairs, and any number of legs.
